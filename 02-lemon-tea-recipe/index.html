Lemon Tea

Let's try below

Ingredients

  • 500ml of water
  • 1-2 lemons (cut them in half)
  • A kitchen knife
  • A small mug to drink
  • Honey or sugar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Put the water to warm until it starts to boil.
  2. When the water is boiling, put the lemon halves inside.
  3. Let it boil for 5 minutes to extract the lemon flavor.
  4. Turn off the heat and wait until it's warm enough to drink.
  5. Pour into your mug and add honey or sugar if desired.
  6. Enjoy your homemade lemon tea!
